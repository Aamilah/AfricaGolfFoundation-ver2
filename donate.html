Donate to the Africa Golf Foundation

The Africa Golf Foundation (AGF) is an organization dedicated to promoting and developing the sport of golf across Africa by increasing accessibility to golf and fostering a vibrant golfing community. Our goals go beyond the sport as we are dedicated not just to grow the sport but to harness the power of the sport to bring real and sustainable change and empower communities.

Equipment Donation

Your old golf club could be the first club that a future champion holds. We accept donations of new and used golf equipment such as clubs, balls, bags, shoes, and other equipment. Your donated equipment will be used to support aspiring golfers and enhance the experience in Africa. Please contact us for a delivery address or, where possible, collection?
