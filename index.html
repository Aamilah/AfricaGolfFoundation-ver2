Creating Opportunities, Changing Lives, Empowering Communities

“Give a man a fish and you will feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish and you will feed him for a lifetime.”

The Africa Golf Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to making golf more inclusive and accessible to communities historically marginalized and excluded from the sport. Our mission is to break down social, economic, and cultural barriers, creating opportunities for individuals of all backgrounds to engage with golf. We strive to inspire a new generation of golfers and other golf-related professionals, promoting diversity and equity within the sport, while helping to empower communities.

Funding

Our grants and sponsorships are designed to assist individuals at every stage of the journey, from aspiring players, to coaches, caddies groundskeepers and officials. We also provide grants to golf clubs seeking to improve their facilities or provide access to underprivileged players, and to other organisations seeking to grow participation in the sport.

Training

We are dedicated to equipping members of Africa's diverse community with the knowledge, skills and resources needed to excel in every aspect of the sport. Through our training initiatives, we seek to empower individuals to achieve their full potential. Our comprehensive training programs cater to individuals across the golf spectrum..

Equipment and Partnerships

Providing much needed equipment to members of the golf community in Africa who are restricted from accessing the game through lack of equipment and seeking partnership opportunities for golf equipment, such as golf clubs, bags, carts, uniforms, etc to improve facilities and the manner in which the game is played in Africa.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make golf accessible to all in Africa, regardless of socio-economic background, by providing financial assistance, mentorship, and resources. We believe in the transformative power of golf to foster camaraderie, discipline, perseverance, and inclusivity, empowering individuals to pursue their passion and potential both on and off the course. Our commitment extends to supporting individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds, whether as players, caddies, coaches, or enthusiasts, while also assisting golf clubs across Africa in developing world-class facilities, youth programs, and community-building initiatives.

Together We Can Make A Difference

Join us in our mission to transform lives through the power of golf, your help make golf accessible to all, provide opportunities for personal growth and help in creating positive change in the community.

Make a Donation

Your cash donations are crucial in fuelling our initiatives across Africa. Every donation drives us closer to our goals. Cash donations fund educational and training scholarships, outreach programs, training camps and workshops, support for new and existing facilities and many other initiatives. Equipment is either distributed to players, clubs and educational institutions. It is also used for outreach programs. Your club could be the first club a future champion ever holds.

Become a Partner

Partnerships are a powerful means to achieve our objectives. We are seeking partnership opportunities with both corporate and other parties from both within and outside the sport.

Partnerships bring more than just financial and physical resources, but also knowledge and experience that can aid our programs and their participants. Whether they are short, medium or long-term in nature, every partnership makes a big difference.

Become a Mentor

We are seeking dedicated volunteers to mentor aspiring participants in various roles including players, caddies, coaches, groundskeepers, administrators and more.

Your donation of your time is priceless and could contribute to the career development of the next African legend. Additionally, we are eager to forge partnerships between golf clubs to expand our reach and impact.

Donate to the Africa Golf Foundation

We believe in the transformative power of golf to inspire, educate, and to build and empower communities. Your generous donations enable us to continue our mission of making golf accessible to individuals of all ages and backgrounds, fostering personal growth and building lasting and life-changing connections both on and off the course.
