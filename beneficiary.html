Individuals

Are you passionate about pursuing a career in golf or perhaps your club or organisation is seeking assistance with infrastructure development or to enhance opportunities for members or local communities? The Africa Golf Foundation is here to help.

We invite individuals from all backgrounds and skill levels to apply for assistance in beginning or enhancing their careers in golf. Whether you are and aspiring player, coach, caddy, groundskeeper, administrator, referee or official, we are here to help you to achieve your goals.

Clubs & Organisations.

Clubs and organisations are encouraged to apply for assistance to enhance opportunities for members and the local community. Whether you are looking to improve facilities, expand youth programs, or promote inclusivity, we are here to lend a helping hand. Our support extends beyond financial assistance to provide guidance, expertise, and resources to help your club or organisation thrive.

Do not let barriers stand in the way of your passions. Apply for support today and take the first step towards a brighter future in golf. Together we can unlock opportunities and create a more inclusive and vibrant golf community.

Follow Us

Africa Golf Foundation is dedicated to developing the golf across the continent, with a mission to make the game more inclusive and accessible. We are breaking down barriers, creating opportunities and changing lives, and empowering entire communities. By fostering a culture of inclusion and growth, Africa Golf Foundation is paving the way for a brighter future, where golf serves as a powerful tool for personal development and community empowerment across the continent.
