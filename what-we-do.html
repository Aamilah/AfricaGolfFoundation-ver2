We are dedicated to changing lives through the power of golf, creating opportunities that empower communities and foster inclusive growth. Our mission is to make the sport accessible to all, driving positive change across the continent.

About Us

The Africa Golf Foundation (AGF) is an organization dedicated to promoting and developing the sport of golf across Africa by increasing accessibility to golf and fostering a vibrant golfing community. Our goals go beyond the sport as we are dedicated not just to grow the sport but to harness the power of the sport to bring real and sustainable change and empower communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to make golf accessible to all in Africa regardless of socio-economic background. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the joys and benefits of golf, from the camaraderie that it fosters to the lessons in discipline perseverance and sportsmanship it imparts. By providing financial assistance, mentorship programs and access to resources, we aim to empower individuals to pursue their passion for golf and realise their full potential both on and off the course.

We believe in the transformative power of golf to break down barriers and foster inclusivity. We are dedicated to providing opportunities to individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds to access and thrive in the world of golf. Whether they aspire to be players, caddies, coaches, groundskeepers, referees and officials, or simply enjoying the game at their local golf club, we are committed to levelling the playing field and ensuring that everyone has a chance to participate.

Furthermore, we recognise the vital role that golf clubs play as hubs of development and community engagement. As such, we extend our support to golf clubs across Africa, providing assistance in creating world class facilities, youth programs and capacity building initiatives.

Through our training and development program, we offer a range of training opportunities, delivered both directly and through trusted partners, designed to enhance the knowledge and abilities of individuals and organisations involved in golf across Africa. We are committed to expanding access to golf and creating opportunities for communities that have been underserved historically. Through our community outreach program, we actively engage with areas that have little to no exposure to golf, raising awareness about the sport's potential and the opportunities that it offers. We regularly organise donation drives to collect both new and used golf equipment. These donations serve a dual purpose: first, they enable us to expand our community outreach efforts by providing equipment for training clinics, camps and other programs that introduce golf to individuals who may have never had access to the sport. Second, we use the donations to support talented individuals who face financial barriers. By redistributing donated equipment to those in need, we ensure that everyone has the opportunity to pursue their golfing dreams, regardless of financial circumstances.

We are dedicated to strengthening golf clubs and facilities by providing vital resources and support.
